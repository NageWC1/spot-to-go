Description

In this project, we would like to develop an Android app, in which users can find popular restaurants in a map. When a user opens the App, the App will show the restaurants based on the user's location. The user can click on the location of restaurants, and then watch the corresponding video published by other people who have already been there before on Tiktok or Youtube.

The steps are as follows:

1\. Load the Google Map on our Android app.

2\. Link the restaurant link to a video.

Once the users click on this restaurant, they could watch the restaurant videos.

In this project, you'll get some basic knowledge of developing app. With experience on Android app, you may get job offer from Google or other Android-based service provider companies.

Requirements

Skills required:

* Basic knowledge of Android programming  
* Basic knowledge of programming skills  
* Jave or Python is a plus
